Project Title One

A digital branding and UX design initiative for a creative tech startup

Overview

This project involved a full brand identity and responsive web design solution for a new-age tech startup. From research to design execution, I owned the entire process.

  • Role: Designer, Developer
  • Tools: Figma, Webflow, Adobe Suite
  • Year: 2025

Process

I started with user research, competitive analysis, and client interviews. Using this insight, I created wireframes, branding guidelines, and high-fidelity mockups before development.

Outcome

The final result is a clean, responsive website and a unique brand identity that positions the client as a creative leader in their field. The site loads in under 1s and saw a 65% boost in engagement.
